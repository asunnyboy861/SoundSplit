## Description
SoundSplit gives you independent control over your iPhone's volume levels.

ONE VOLUME KEY. THREE DIFFERENT SOUNDS.
Your iPhone has one volume button, but it controls ringer, media, and calls differently. SoundSplit lets you create Volume Profiles that set each one independently so your alarm always wakes you, your meetings stay quiet, and your music sounds great.

CREATE VOLUME PROFILES
- Home: Ringer 80%, Media 60%, Calls 70%
- Office: Ringer 30%, Media 20%, Calls 50%
- Sleep: Ringer 10%, Media 0%, Calls 30%
- Outdoor: Ringer 100%, Media 80%, Calls 90%
Create unlimited custom profiles with your own icons and names.

SWITCH WITH ONE TAP
Tap any profile card to instantly switch your volume configuration. No more fumbling with volume buttons in meetings or missing calls because you muted everything.

QUICK ACTIONS
- Mute All: Silence everything instantly
- Max Volume: Full volume in one tap

FOCUS MODE INTEGRATION (Pro)
Link volume profiles to Focus Modes. When you turn on Work focus, SoundSplit automatically switches to your Office profile. Sleep focus? Your Sleep profile activates. No manual switching needed.

SHORTCUTS SUPPORT (Pro)
Create custom Shortcuts that switch volume profiles. Combine with automations for time-based or location-based volume switching.

CONTROL CENTER WIDGET (Pro)
Access your favorite profiles right from Control Center. No need to open the app.
