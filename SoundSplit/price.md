## Why No Lifetime Purchase
1. Ongoing costs: Focus Mode integration, Shortcuts support, Live Activity require continuous iOS version adaptation
2. Feature iteration: Per-App auto volume, iCloud sync require ongoing development and server costs
3. User expectations: Lifetime purchase users expect permanent free updates, unsustainable
4. Industry trend: 2025-2026 iOS utility apps mainstream model is subscription-based
