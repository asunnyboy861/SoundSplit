Common Questions

How do Volume Profiles work?

Each profile stores separate volume levels for Ringer & Alerts, Media & Apps, and Phone Calls. Tap any profile card to instantly switch your volume settings.

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, or open the App Store and tap your profile picture. You can cancel or change your subscription at any time.

How do I restore purchases?

Open SoundSplit → Settings → Restore Purchases. This will sync your subscription across devices using the same Apple ID.

Why can't I control each volume independently?

iOS manages volume at the system level. SoundSplit works by creating profiles that you switch between, each with your preferred volume mix. Focus Mode and Shortcuts can automate switching for you.
