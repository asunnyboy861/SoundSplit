SoundSplit

Independent control over your iPhone's volume levels.

One Volume Key. Three Different Sounds.

Your iPhone has one volume button, but it controls ringer, media, and calls differently. SoundSplit lets you create Volume Profiles that set each one independently.

Volume Profiles - Save custom configurations for Home, Office, Sleep, and Outdoor scenarios.
One-Tap Switch - Instantly switch between profiles without fumbling with volume buttons.
Focus Mode Integration - Automatically switch profiles when you change Focus Modes.
Shortcuts Support - Create automations for time-based or location-based volume switching.
