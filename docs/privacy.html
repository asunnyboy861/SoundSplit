Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

SoundSplit is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Volume Profiles: Stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not access, transmit, or store your profile data on our servers.
  • Subscription Status: Managed by Apple through StoreKit 2. We only know whether you have an active subscription, not your payment details.
  • Feedback Messages: If you use the Contact Support feature, your message, email, and name are sent to our support system to help resolve your issue.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (except when you voluntarily provide it via Contact Support)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos or media files
  • Browsing history
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Device advertising identifiers

3. Data Storage

All volume profile data is stored locally on your device. If you subscribe to SoundSplit Pro and enable iCloud sync (future feature), data will be encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account, which we cannot access.

4. Third-Party Services

SoundSplit uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Feedback messages are retained for up to 12 months for support purposes, then permanently deleted. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

SoundSplit does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
